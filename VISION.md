# 💎 Tile & Granite E-commerce Visualizer: The Vision

## 🌟 Project Overview
This project is a high-end, interactive visualization platform specifically designed for the **Tile and Granite E-commerce industry**. It bridges the gap between seeing a static product image and understanding how a material will truly look inside a home.

## 🎯 Core Vision: "See it, Feel it, Believe it"
The primary goal is to empower customers by providing a **Live Material Preview**. Instead of guessing if a specific granite slab or ceramic tile matches their aesthetic, users can virtually "install" it in high-fidelity room settings.

---

## 🚀 The User Journey

### 1. Room Selection
Users start by choosing a room that best represents their own space—whether it's a **Modern Bathroom**, a **Contemporary Kitchen**, or a **Minimalist Living Area**.

### 2. High-Fidelity Visualization
Upon entering the visualizer, the user is presented with a 3D-mapped environment. This is the heart of the experience:
- **Interactive Hotspots**: Users can click directly on walls or floors within the image to activate selection modes.
- **Glassmorphic UI**: A premium, clean sidebar that doesn't distract from the visual result.

### 3. Live Texture Testing
The core engine supports:
- **Instant Texture Swapping**: Clicking any tile from the catalog immediately renders it onto the room surfaces.
- **Surface Lighting & Perspective**: Intelligent layering that maintains the original shadows, reflections, and perspective of the room.
- **Mode Toggles**: Seamlessly switch between testing **Walls** and **Floors** to create a complete, harmonious design.
